## ğŸ¯ Pro Tips

### Beginner Tips
1. **Position Early**: Move basket to predicted landing spot before fruit arrives
2. **Center Position**: Start near the middle of the screen
3. **Smooth Movements**: Don't jerk the basket, make smooth motions
4. **Focus**: Watch the entire screen, not just your basket

### Intermediate Tips
1. **Anticipate Patterns**: Fruits spawn randomly, but watch for clusters
2. **Stay Mobile**: Keep moving to catch multiple fruits in quick succession
3. **Edge Awareness**: Don't let fruits reach the bottom
4. **Speed Management**: As difficulty increases, use quicker, smaller movements

### Advanced Tips
1. **Memorize Zones**: Know the screen width to catch fruit at edges
2. **Optimize Movement**: Every pixel counts at higher levels
3. **Predict Spawn**: Random but uniform - watch for timing patterns
4. **Stay Calm**: Panic at high levels leads to mistakes
